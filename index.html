Quoted shares

Quarter-up: lower + ¼ × (higher − lower)

Bargains average: (highest bargain + lowest bargain) ÷ 2

Use the lower of the two. Divide pence by 100 to convert to pounds.

Lifetime CLT

Available NRB = £325,000 − earlier CLTs in previous 7 years.

Trustees pay: 20%. Donor pays: 20/80 = 25%.

GCT = net CLT + donor-paid lifetime tax.

Death tax on a gift

Transfer − available NRB → ×40% → taper relief → less lifetime IHT.

Trustees paid: use net CLT. Donor paid: use GCT.

Dates

Payment / interest: end of month of death + 6 months.

IHT account: end of month of death + 12 months.

Death estate: legally due on account delivery; interest runs from the six-month date.

Instalments

Qualifying assets: land/buildings and certain shares.

Annual instalment = total IHT × qualifying asset ÷ total estate ÷ 10.
